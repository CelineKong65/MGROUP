ABOUT THE PROPERTY SHOP

Our Property Shop's History

2005

In August 2005, Property Store was established in Malacca. Initially, our shop was not well known, but with increasing demand for rental properties, we became the only property seller offering this service. Our business grew rapidly as a result.

2012

2012 was challenging due to the opening of branches in Kuala Lumpur and Johor. Despite a significant drop in sales during the outbreak, our store maintained a comprehensive range of property services, attracting many customers.

2019

In 2019, the epidemic led to a significant drop in sales. We launched a website, facilitating online property inquiries, which have continued to grow in popularity as people seek properties without going out.

Our Vision and Mission

We aim to provide customers with the highest quality properties and office spaces for living, work, and creativity. We also strive to offer excellent customer service and a great shopping experience. Our goal is to be the community's preferred property store and a trusted supplier of properties and office spaces.
